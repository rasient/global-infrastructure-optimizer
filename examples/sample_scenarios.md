# Sample Scenarios

## Scenario 1 — Bike-to-Transit Upgrade

Increase:

- walking / cycling investment
- transfer / interface investment

Expected result:

- higher accessibility
- better interface quality
- stronger system resilience

## Scenario 2 — Transit Priority Corridors

Increase:

- transit priority

Expected result:

- stronger mode portfolio
- better coordination
- reduced bus/tram delay sensitivity

## Scenario 3 — Car Pressure Stress Test

Increase:

- car pressure

Expected result:

- lower coordination
- weaker accessibility
- lower resilience if alternatives are not strong

## Scenario 4 — Small Tram Feeder

Use the small tram as a missing-middle mode.

Expected role:

- feeder to metro
- upgrade from bus on medium-density corridors
- structured but cheaper than full tram expansion
